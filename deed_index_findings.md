## Verdicts on user's hypotheses

1. **"Property went to Clear Sky via tax auction" — NO.** [Confirmed]
   The transfer from Hawkins to Clear Sky Trust was a **private estate sale** (Personal-Representative deed → reshuffled through MITTEN REI LLC → Hawkins Trust → Clear Sky Trust on 8/29/2022, $75,000 with $67,500 seller-financing). It was not a county tax-foreclosure auction. The Hawkins family had multiple historic tax issues (1992, 2001, 2017, March 2022), but in every case the delinquencies were redeemed before forfeiture matured into county ownership.

   Robert Lee Hawkins death certificate recorded 6/22/2022 (Document 2022-024562). Probate was opened in Ingham County; **Robert L Hawkins II** served as Personal Representative; **Charles Hawkins** is named as a successor co-trustee on the trust. Public-records databases (voterrecords.com, simplecontacts) showing him at "age 85/86" appear to be stale data.
